Let's get acquainted!

Visions Festival is an annual music festival that takes place in Sacramento, California.

Visions Festival is a unique mix of R&B, hip hop, electronic, dream pop, juzz, and rock.

Visions Festival is 8 years of never-ending energy, passion, positive vibes, freedom, diversity and most importantly, live music!

With 5 stages, and 100+ artists from all over the world, every Visions lineup makes hands clap, hearts beat, heads nod, and crowds holler.
